about me

I am a creative based in London with background in design and engineering. I believe deeply that art and science are far more closely related than we imagine and are the two most important tools we have to help us understand the universe and make it a better place. I am passionate about design, technology, sustainability and challenging the status quo. I appreciate simplicity and accesibility in design and always strive for these in my work. Minimalist, vegan, runner and photography enthusiat.  I am always on the lookout for new challenges and exciting projects to get involved in.

Open Source Product Design

My MA thesis at Goldsmiths, University of London. Exhibited at London Design Festival 2016. The project proposes open-source products, based on the open and collaborative design of a laboratory equipment for medical research. The syringe pump for microfluidics, as an example of using emerging technologies as force for good, proposes a vision of what product design could look like in the future. Read more
